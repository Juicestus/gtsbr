337 What is a computer network?

A set of independent computer systems interconnected by telecommunication links.

337 What is a node?

An individual computer on a network.

338 What is a switched, dial up telephone link?

A connection using a telephone line using a call and hang up system, manually routed to each node.

339 What is bandwidth?

The rete information could be sent or received.

340 What is broadband?

A communication link exceeding 256,000 bits per second.

340 What is a digital subscriber line?

Using a telephone line at a different frequency to transmit an internet connection.

340 What is Ethernet?

Developed by Xerox in the mid 70s, a cable that can transmit up to 10 million bits per second. The form factor for all Ethernets.

341 What is Fast Ethernet?

Improved Ethernet to transmit 100 Megabits per second.

341 What is gigabit networking?

Transmitting over a billion bits per second.

341 What is Gigabit Ethernet?

Ethernet that can transmit up to 100 Gigabits per second.

341 What is the IEEE?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

342 What is wireless data communication?

Using a wireless technology to transmit data.

342 What is mobile computing?

Nodes that connect wirelessly to the network.

342 Wat is a Wireless Local Area Network?

WLAN, A user transmits wirelessly to a modem, which connects through the DSL to the Internet.

343 What is WIFI?

Wireless Fidelity. IEEE 802.11 wireless network standard. A warless communication from a hot spot to a range of around 150-300 feet.

343 What is a personal area network (PAN)?

Privately owned interconnected devices in close proximity.

343 What is a metropolitan area network (MAN)?

A network whose scope is much larger than that a WIFI.

344 What is a wide area network (WAN)?

A network using a telecommunication tower to transmit data many miles away.

344 What is a local area network (LAN)?

A network that connects hardware devices such as printers, computers, and storage on a local network.

346 What is bus topology?

Devices connected to a main wire.

346 What is ring topology?

Devices connected to every device through their neighbors.

346 What is star topology?

Devices connected to a center node.

347 What is a share cable?

A fiber optic cable strung around a building to connect nodes.

347 What is a repeater?

A device that amplifies and forwards a signal.

347 What is a bridge?

A device that can send a message to a specific node.

348 What is a switch?

A device that is the center node for many wires and manages transmission between them.

349 What is a dedicated point to point line?

A link that connects two machines.

349 What is a store and forward, packet switched technology?

A system where a message goes through nodes until it gets to its destination.

349 What is a packet?

A unit of information of the maximum transmittable size.
